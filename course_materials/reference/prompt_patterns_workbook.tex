\section{Pattern 4 — Critique and Improve}
Ask the model to score its own work. Then ask it to improve the weak parts.

\begin{patternbox}
\textbf{Pattern:}\\
Critique the result. Identify weaknesses. Improve the result based on the critique.
\end{patternbox}
