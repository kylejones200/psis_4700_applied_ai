# Week 7 — Applied Project Workshop (STEW-style Lesson Plan)

- Audience: Adult undergraduates (hybrid/online)
- Duration: 3 hours contact + 1–2 hours prep

## Overview
Hands-on workshop to advance capstone projects. Teams refine scope, test assumptions, and prepare for final demos.

## Learning Objectives
- Frame a feasible, ethical, and valuable problem.
- Define acceptance criteria and evaluation plan.
- Reduce project risk via targeted experiments.

## Materials & Tools
- Slides: `slides_intro/week-07-project-workshop.md`
- Project template (problem, metrics, risks, milestones)
- Checklist: rehearsal, contingency, submission

## Key Vocabulary
- MVP, acceptance criteria, risk burndown, contingency, runbook

## Prerequisites
- Weeks 1–6; project idea and initial materials

## Schedule (3:00)
- 0:00–0:10 Recap & goals
- 0:10–0:30 Project checkpoint: status + top 3 risks
- 0:30–0:55 Hypothesis + experiment design
- 0:55–1:05 Break
- 1:05–1:40 Work time: run a quick test; gather evidence
- 1:40–2:10 Define acceptance criteria and demo story
- 2:10–2:35 Rehearsal dry runs in pairs; feedback
- 2:35–2:55 Submission checklist walkthrough
- 2:55–3:00 Exit ticket

## Warm-Up (10 min)
- Prompt: “What is the one result that will make your demo credible?”

## Core Activities
- Risk burndown: plan to retire top risks this week.
- Experiment design: falsifiable hypotheses and quick tests.
- Rehearsal: narrative, timing, backup plan.

## Differentiation
- Provide technical/non-technical experiment examples.
- Offer optional 1:1 instructor consults.

## Assessment
- Formative: Exit ticket stating one risk retired and next step.
- Summative: Updated project brief + rehearsal video (optional).

## Homework/Prep (60–90 min)
- Finish quick tests; polish materials; finalize demo.

## Instructor Notes
- Keep teams focused on outcomes, not tooling.
- Timebox coaching; use a visible timer and checkpoints.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Allow alternative demo formats; ensure captioning resources.

## References
- MVP playbooks; demo storytelling resources.
