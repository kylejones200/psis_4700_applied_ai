## Why This Course Exists

Artificial intelligence already shapes decisions that affect daily life  
Many people use AI tools without understanding how they work  
This course closes that gap

> This course addresses the growing demand for AI literacy across all disciplines. Students from any major can learn to work with AI tools effectively without needing advanced mathematics or programming skills.
---

## What Applied AI Means Here

Applied AI focuses on use, not theory  
Models matter only when they support decisions  
Code matters only when it runs and produces insight

> Applied AI focuses on using existing tools and techniques to solve real problems rather than building models from scratch. This practical orientation makes AI accessible to practitioners who need results, not just researchers.
---

## What This Course Is Not

This course does not prove theorems  
This course does not train large models from scratch  
This course does not treat AI as magic

> This course won't teach you to build neural networks from scratch or derive mathematical proofs. It's designed for practitioners who need to use AI effectively in their work, not aspiring AI researchers.
---

## Who This Course Is For

This course serves students across majors  
No advanced math background is required

> This course serves students across majors - business, humanities, sciences, engineering. No advanced math background is required. Curiosity and persistence matter more than prior experience.
