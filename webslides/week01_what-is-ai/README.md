# Week 1: What Is AI?

## Overview
Introduction to AI history, core definitions, current capabilities/limits, and responsible use. Students experience simple demos and reflect on AI in their field.

## Learning Objectives
- Define AI, ML, and DL in plain language
- Distinguish symbolic vs statistical vs generative approaches
- Identify opportunities/risks of AI in students' domains
- Practice critical evaluation of AI demos
