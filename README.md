## üìö What's Inside

### Introduction to Applied AI (8-Week Course)
A gateway course designed for students with no prior AI experience. Covers fundamental concepts, real-world applications, ethics, and hands-on experience building simple AI systems.

**Weekly Topics:**
1. What is AI? History, definitions, and impact
2. Data and Learning: How AI systems learn from data
3. Applications Across Domains: Case studies in healthcare, business, creative fields
4. Ethics and Policy: Bias, privacy, transparency, and governance
5. Generative & Conversational Models: LLMs, chatbots, and prompt engineering
6. Deployment & Integration: Taking AI from prototype to production
7. Applied Project Workshop: Team-based problem solving
8. Capstone Presentations: Student demos and reflections

### Advanced AI Topics
Technical deep-dives for students ready to explore implementation details:
- Machine Learning Foundations
- Natural Language Processing
- Computer Vision
- Time Series Analysis
- Responsible AI & Safety
- System Integration & MLOps

### Special Topics Library
